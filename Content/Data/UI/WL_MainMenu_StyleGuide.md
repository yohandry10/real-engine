# World Leader Main Menu Style Guide

## Colors
- Petroleum blue: `#0C2333`
- Deep blue: `#102B3F`
- Amber: `#C89B3C`
- Soft gold: `#E0B85A`
- Tactical white: `#E9EEF2`
- Steel gray: `#77828C`
- Dark gray: `#1A1F24`
- Alert red: `#A64444`

## Typography
- Title/logo: Bahnschrift SemiBold Condensed or Barlow Condensed.
- UI: Segoe UI / Inter / Roboto Condensed.
- Rules: uppercase for primary nav, no negative letter spacing, no sci-fi novelty fonts.

## Iconography
- Minimal line icons: globe, star/command, folder, users, gear, blocks, power.
- Preferred future set: Lucide or custom single-weight tactical glyphs exported as SVG/texture.

## Layout
- 16:9 first. Safe zones: 72 px at 4K, 36 px at 1080p.
- Button height: 76 px at 4K.
- Button radius: 6 px.
- Panel radius: 8 px.
- Borders: 1-2 px steel/gold.

## Motion
- MVP: static hero with hover/focus transitions.
- Phase 2: 1-2 percent slow background zoom, subtle route pulses, dust/haze particles, non-loop-breaking ambient.
- Avoid: arcade flashing, busy HUD sweep lines, aggressive cyberpunk neon.
